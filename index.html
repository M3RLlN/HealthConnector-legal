HealthConnector

Legal documents · Bachelor thesis project · Hochschule RheinMain

HealthConnector is a non-commercial Bachelor thesis project. It is a small, single-user web application that aggregates the developer's own personal fitness and health data from multiple wearables (currently Huawei devices via the Health Service Kit and a Pixel Watch via the Google Health API) into a private local database, for self-tracking and academic research.

This site only hosts the legal documents required by the third-party providers from whom data is read. The application itself is not published here.
